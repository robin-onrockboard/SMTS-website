About SMTS

Smart Move Trade
Solutions Inc

Smart Move Trade Solutions Inc (SMTS) was founded in June 2024 and is a cross-border supply chain services company based in Boston, Massachusetts. We are currently one of Temu's official partner warehouses in Boston.

Since our founding, SMTS has leveraged a professional local operations team and highly efficient warehousing and fulfillment capabilities to build a stable, reliable US supply chain infrastructure for cross-border e-commerce sellers, enabling seamless end-to-end fulfillment from origin to the American consumer's doorstep.

To date, SMTS operates a 20,000 sqft professional warehouse facility with a 25-person local team, serving over 80+ platform sellers across major e-commerce platforms including Temu, Shein, TikTok Shop, Etsy, and Shopify. We have processed over 2.3 million shipments, with peak daily order volume reaching 17,000 orders.

Milestones

From Zero to One, Growing Steadily

April 2024
SMTS Officially Founded
Smart Move Trade Solutions Inc was incorporated in Massachusetts, USA, and began building its East Coast warehousing infrastructure.
June 2024
Warehouse Built & Operations Launched
The 20,000 sqft Boston warehouse was fully constructed and operational, with a 25-person local team in place, ready to onboard the first wave of sellers.
September 2024
Rapid Seller Onboarding
Sellers from Temu, Shein, TikTok Shop, Etsy, Shopify and other platforms onboarded in volume. Daily order volume rose rapidly as warehouse operations hit their stride.
February 2025
Became Temu Official Partner Warehouse
After rigorous certification by the Temu platform, SMTS was officially designated as one of Temu's partner warehouses in Boston, offering priority local fulfillment services for Temu sellers.
June 2025
Multi-Platform System Integration
Completed deep integration with LingXing and JiFeng WMS systems, enabling fully automated multi-platform order synchronization. Warehouse SKUs surpassed 70,000+.
2026
Total Shipments Surpass 2.3 Million
Serving over 80 sellers with peak daily order volume reaching 17,000 orders. Continuing to expand warehousing capacity and helping more brands reach the US market.
